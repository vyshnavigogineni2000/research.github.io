Computational Intelligence and Machine Learning (CIML) Research Lab:


Welcome to the Computational Intelligence and Machine Learning (CIML) Lab! The goal of our research is to analyze and develop novel machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data science-based systems and architectures.

The CI Lab provides many scientific activities. We conduct scientific research projects, publish scientific research papers, deliver monthly research talks, supervise PhD, MSc and bachelor theses, teach many courses, publish books & book chapters, apply to patents, and organize scientific conferences and workshops. Currently, there are 21 graduate/undergraduate students and researchers working in the CIML lab. We had 39 former researchers/students.

Our research area is related to the algorithmic and theoretical background of machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data science including deep learning, pattern recognition with applications to cancer research, bioinformatics, computer vision, natural language processing, stability and convergence analysis, decision support systems, recommender systems and more specific tasks.

The National Science Foundation (NSF), National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA), Arkansas Tech University (ATU), ITEA4 Program, Scientific and Technological Research Council of Turkey (TUBITAK), Istanbul University-Cerrahpasa, and Council of Higher Education have awarded our research projects.

We cooperate with different disciplines and colleges/departments like college of medicine, college of sociology, and other potential colleges/departments. Interdisciplinary collaborations are always encouraged and important for our studies.

For more information, please contact us at tensari@atu.edu.

We hope to see you in the CIML Lab!
